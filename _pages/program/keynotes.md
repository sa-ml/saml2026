## Keynote Speaker — Jan Bosch

<p class="speaker-bio">Professor of Software Engineering, Director Software Center, Chalmers University of Technology; Professor of AI Engineering, Eindhoven University of Technology</p>

**Title:** _Towards AI-driven systems that continuously learn_

**Abstract**

With the emergence of generative and agentic AI, the way we engineer software-intensive systems and how these systems operate is fundamentally changing. In this talk, I explore the implications of Generative AI and Agentic AI on engineering and the transition of software-intensive systems from static artifacts to continuously learning and evolving systems.
